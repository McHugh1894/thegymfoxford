WHY US?

Move more live longer

Opening Hours

The gym is open for seventeen hours a day Monday to Friday and twelve hours a day on the weekends. Plenty of time to choose from.

Affordable

At 29euro a month we are a very affordable gym. We also have a yearly option for 259euro.

No Contracts

Our membership runs monthly. We wont tie you down to long contracts like other gyms. You can cancel anytime with no penalty.

The Gym

Equipment

Our gym is fully equipped with free weights and machines. We have barbells, dumbbells, power racks, treadmills, bikes, assault bikes and cross trainers.

Staff

Our friendly staff are availbe during all open hours for your assistance. They will help you with whatever needs be.

Location

We are in a rural location but also very accesible from Foxford. There is plenty of on site parking available and its free.

Services Available

Group Classes

All classes are free with your membership. We have a number of classes to choose from such as circuits, conditioning, spinning, pilates and yoga.

One to One training

We have a five personal trainers in our gym available for one to one training. One session is free per month with your membership.

Physiotherapy

We have a physiotherapist on site so if you have any injuries need seeing to they can take care of it. There is two free sessions per month included with membership.
